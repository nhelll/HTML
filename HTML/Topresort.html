YUMIYA TOP RESORT

Yumiya in Bontoc is a cozy resort and events place located in Bontoc, Southern Leyte. It’s known for its relaxing ambiance, offering cottages, a swimming pool, and open spaces perfect for family gatherings, outings with friends, and special celebrations. The place combines a refreshing natural setting with modern amenities, making it a nice spot to unwind and enjoy leisure time away from the busy town center.

Yumeya Mountain Resort delivers a peaceful, Japan-inspired escape with stunning views and comfortable accommodations—all wrapped in a scenic upland setting in Southern Leyte. Whether you're looking for a unique staycation or a tranquil stopover during your travels, Yumeya has a distinct vibe worth experiencing.
